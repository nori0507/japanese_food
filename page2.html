Japanese Regional Cuisine

This is a page about Japanese regional cuisine.

Regional cuisine are very unique and may different from Japanese cuisine that you would expect.

What determines regional cuisines?

There are some components such as...

  • Weather
  • Trade
  • Food Availability
  • Cooking Traditions
