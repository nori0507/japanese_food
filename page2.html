Japanese Regional Cuisine

This is a page about Japanese regional cuisine.

Regional cuisine are very unique and may different from Japanese cuisine that you would expect.

What determines regional cuisines?

  • Weather
  • Trade
  • Food Availability
  • Cooking Traditions

Famous Japanese regional cuisine

  1. Jingisukan - grilled lamb dish(Hokkaido).
  2. Unaju - grilled eel served over rice in a lacquered box(Shizuoka).
  3. Goya Champuru - stir-fry with bitter melon, tofu, pork, and egg(Okinawa Prefecture).
